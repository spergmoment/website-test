About it

What is it?

Law Enforcer is a simple discord.py bot I made. It's not very unique, only having pretty basic moderation commands for now, but it will branch out in the future.

Why did you make it?

I made this bot as a project to help me relearn Python, as well as expand my knowledge of the Discord API.
